About CICS

The CICS at NISU offers programs in Computer Science, Information Technology and Information Science, aimed at developing skilled professionals in the IT industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What programs are offered at CICS?

CICS offers Bachelor's degrees in Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and Bachelor of Library and Information Science.


How can I enroll in CICS?

To enroll in CICS, visit the NISU website and follow the admission guidelines provided there.

For more information about CICS, visit their official website: CICS Official Website.


Goals of the Institute of Information and Computer Studies


The IICS endeavors to:

1. train students to become computer professionals knowledgeable and competent in meeting the growing demand for software developers and IT persons in government, business, industry and education;

2. harness and undertake relevant ICT researches to provide valuable services and to share expertise and facilities with various stakeholders on the transfer and promotion of information and communications technology for local, regional and national benefit; and

3. inculcate in its students positive Filipino values and attitudes as well as the value of independent and life-long learning.

Program Objectives for Bachelor of Science in Computer Science


At the completion of the program, the graduates should have:

1. acquired computing concepts and theories, algorithmic foundations and new developments in computing to design and develop software solutions;

2. equipped with adequate preparations, practicum programs, field exposures and related learning experiences to meet future needs in the field of computing;

3. engaged in research and collaborative work with the industry to ensure the practical application of computing technologies;

4. imparted technology transfer through extension activities in the hope that it will improve the lives of the community it serves;

5. developed teamwork and interpersonal skills, social responsibilities and participation to local, regional and nation building.

Program Objectives for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology


At the completion of the program, the graduates should have:

1. acquired the necessary skills in the utilization of both software and hardware technologies in the selection, development, application, integration and management of computing resources within an organization;

2. equipped with adequate preparations, practicum programs, field exposures and related learning experiences to meet future needs in the field of computing;

3. engaged in research and collaborative work with the industry to ensure the practical application of computing technologies;

4. imparted technology transfer through extension activities in the hope that it will improve the lives of the community it serves;

5. developed teamwork and interpersonal skills, social responsibilities and participation to local, regional and nation building.

Program Offerings


  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)

    • A 4-year program emphasizing advanced computational theories and cutting-edge applications in artificial intelligence, software engineering, and data science.


  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

    • Web & Mobile Development & Network Security

    Focuses on the design, development, and management of information systems to enhance business processes and decision-making.


  • Bachelor of Science and Library Science (BLIS)

    • Develops practical expertise in network management, cloud computing, and software solutions for diverse industries.

Other Meaning of CICS


CICS stands for Customer Information Control System, and it's an online transaction processing (OLTP) system that runs on IBM's z/OS mainframe operating system. CICS is a mixed-language application server that acts as middleware between the z/OS operating system and business applications. Read more...


CICS is used by many companies in industries like banking, retail, insurance, and transportation for mission-critical applications like credit card processing and banking transactions. CICS is known for its reliability and ability to process large volumes of work quickly. IBM claims that CICS can process over 1.1 million transactions per second. Read more...

Schools with CICS


  • Northern Iloilo State University

    • The College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS) at the University of Santo Tomas is a leader in technological education and innovation in the Philippines. CICS is renowned for its state-of-the-art programs in Computer Science, Information Systems, and Information Technology, equipping students with the skills to excel in the modern digital landscape.


  • University of Santo Tomas

    • The College of Information and Computing Sciences was formally established in 2014, with its separation as an independent academic unit from the Faculty of Engineering. Its programs trace their histories to the College of Science (for Computer Science), the College of Commerce and Business Administration (for Information Systems), and the Faculty of Engineering (for Information Technology).


  • Lyceum Northwestern University

    • The College of Information and Computing Studies of Lyceum-Northwestern University was established on June 2009. The office of the college is located at the 1st floor of Florencia Building and (4) computer laboratories are also situated at the same location. Two (2) more computer laboratories are positioned at the 2nd floor of the same building.


  • University of Southeastern Philippines

    • This College of Information and Computing is committed towards the growth of Southern Philippines by developing information technology professionals, entrepreneurs, managers, and new technologies. During its initial implementation last June 26, 1997, the Institute came up with a plan to prepare the University as a Nodal Training Center in Region XI. Consequently, it grew so smooth because of the support given by the neighboring state universities and colleges.


  • Marinduque State College

    • The College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS) is one of the premiere schools in the province for IT education and services.


  • Western Mindanao State University

    • The college shall provide academic excellence in the field of Information and Communication Technology, with emphasis on the following goals.


  • De La Salle University

    • The College of Information and Computer Studies imparts quality education to its students by equipping them with multiple levels of skills and knowledge in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology through relevant applications and research-based activities.


  • Cagayan State University Carig Campus Learning Environment Network System

    • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) · Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) · Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS).


  • Mindanao State University

    • The College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS) is an academic unit at Mindanao State University Marawi (MSU Marawi) that focuses on computing programs. In 2021, the college has renamed the College of Information Technology and is headed by a College Dean.
