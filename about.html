About Me

Jolan Clark A. Sorillo

I'm a driven individual with a multifaceted approach to life. My passions encompass both physical and digital pursuits, creating a dynamic balance in my personal and professional development. I find immense satisfaction in the sport of badminton, where agility and strategic thinking converge to create a thrilling experience. Beyond the court, I am deeply interested in technology and its potential to connect and inspire. As an avid consumer of online content, I particularly enjoy live streams and videos that explore a wide range of topics, from gaming to culinary arts with proper YouTube tutorial. My dedication to technology extends to my own creative endeavors, where I engage in web development, building simple websites to both practice my skills and bring my ideas to fruition. Furthermore, I am a food enthusiast, always eager to explore new flavors and culinary experiences.

Currently, I am pursuing a degree at Northern Iloilo State University's (NISU) College of Information and Computing Studies (CICS), further demonstrating my commitment to the field of technology. In essence, I am a multifaceted individual who finds joy in a diverse range of activities, from physical challenges to creative pursuits. My passion for technology, combined with my appreciation for both physical and intellectual stimulation, fuels my desire to learn, grow, and contribute to the world around me.
